About Us

GOTypingTest.com

GOTypingTest.com (operated under the brand "Type&Earn") is an online platform built for one simple purpose: to help people become faster, more accurate typists — and to reward that skill in real, measurable ways.

What We Do

We run skill-based typing contests where users compete on speed and accuracy. Unlike games of chance, every result on our platform is a direct outcome of the player's own typing skill — nothing is left to luck. Alongside contests, we offer free practice tests tailored to popular competitive government exams (SSC CHSL, RRB NTPC, DSSSB JSA, and others), so students preparing for these exams can practice with realistic passages and timing.

Our Mission

Typing is a foundational skill for millions of students and job-seekers in India, especially those preparing for government exams that include a typing test component. Our mission is to make that practice engaging, measurable, and — for those who want to test themselves competitively — rewarding.

How It Works

  • Create a free account in seconds (Google Sign-In or name + mobile number).
  • Practice for free using our exam-specific typing passages, or join a paid skill-based contest.
  • Track your words-per-minute (WPM), accuracy, and progress over time from your dashboard.
  • Compete on the leaderboard and win prizes based purely on your typing performance.
