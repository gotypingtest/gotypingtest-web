Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GOTypingTest.com

Is GOTypingTest.com free to use?

Yes. Registration is free, and our Exams practice section is completely free to use. Contests have an entry fee shown before you join, and you play to win a share of the prize pool.

Is this gambling?

No. All contests are games of skill — your result depends entirely on your own typing speed and accuracy, not on chance. Rankings and prizes are calculated purely from your performance.

Do I need to create a password?

No. You can sign in instantly with your Google account, or register with just your name and mobile number — no password or OTP required.

How do I add money to my wallet?

Go to the Wallet page, click "Generate QR & Pay", scan the QR code with any UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.), pay the amount, then submit your Transaction ID (UTR). Our team verifies and credits your wallet, usually within a few hours.

How do withdrawals work?

From the Wallet page, request a withdrawal with your UPI ID or bank account details. The amount is held from your wallet immediately, and we manually transfer it to your account, typically within 24 hours.

What is the minimum age to play contests?

You must be 18 or older to join paid contests, add money, or withdraw winnings.

What happens if I lose internet connection during a contest?

Your result up to the point of disconnection is saved. Entry fees are non-refundable for connectivity issues on your end — please ensure a stable connection before joining a timed contest.

Can I use the same paragraph twice?

Each contest and each numbered practice test is designed to use a specific typing passage, so you get varied content across different tests.

How do I contact support?

Visit our Contact Us page or email gotypingtestgmail.com.
