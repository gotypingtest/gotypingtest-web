1️⃣
Home Row Position
Left hand on A-S-D-F, Right hand on J-K-L-; — yahi starting position hai. F aur J pe bumps hote hain bina dekhe feel karo.
2️⃣
Har Ungli ka Kaam
Har finger sirf apne assigned keys dabati hai. Keyboard mat dekho — screen dekho. Shuru mein slow chalega, practice se speed aayegi.
3️⃣
Accuracy Pehle, Speed Baad Mein
Pehle 95%+ accuracy achieve karo. Speed automatically aayegi. Galat typing ki aadat padh jaati hai jo baad mein mushkil se jaati hai.
